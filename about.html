Lore

A deep dive into the world of BOF

The world of BOF is known as "Heinz" a chaotic and evil world overun by beans and ruled by the true Supreme Bean. Adventurers are challenged with traversing this hectic environment to over-rule the Supreme Bean.

The main protagonist in this narrative is Sean Bean, a world-wide known actor who's career has been cooked by meme culture. This... is his story, his story for redemption and reclaiming his fame and fortune.

Locations

Sean will have a whole world to achieve his goals. Countless enemies to battle, all within the regions of Heinz:

Kidney Caves - The starting area of this adventure.

Pinto Fields - A sparse grassland full of green and nature.

Broad Mountains - A trecherous path for the bold.

Lentil Forest - The realm of the lost, a labrynth of trees.

Cannellini Plaza - A waterside frontage, with great views.

Fava Castle - The oldest citadel in Heinz.

Soy Realm - The Supreme Beans lair, do not enter!

Chickpea Village - A happy village, with a shop :)
